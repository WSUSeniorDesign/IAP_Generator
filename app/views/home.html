IAP Generator

During the deployment of an Incident Command System (ICS), and Incident Action Plan (IAP) is used to lay out the overall plan for managing all aspects of the incident. This is an application for generating Incident Application Plans for FEMA ICS/NIMS incidents built using Express and MongoDB, designed and developed as part of the senior-year capstone project course for the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Wright State University.
